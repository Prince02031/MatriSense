# Evidence Library & Public Sources

### Clinical Disclaimer

> **Clinical Grounding & Accountability**
> *These references informed MatriSense’s conservative triage and guideline-grounded guidance design. MatriSense is not clinically validated, does not diagnose disease, and does not replace doctors or health workers.*

To ensure clinical safety and clear boundaries:
*   **RAG does not decide urgency:** The retrieval process and generative model do not compute the risk status of the mother.
*   **Rule engine controls risk level:** The deterministic, audited clinical rule engine processes confirmed symptoms to output the risk classification (LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH) and safety flags.
*   **Human in the loop:** Health workers and registered doctors retain absolute final authority over all medical choices, hospital assignments, and emergency referrals.
*   **Demo Data Notice:** This demonstration platform uses exclusively synthetic or test case profiles and mock health worker profiles, unless otherwise specified under formal research and data governance approval.
