# MatriSense
### Bangla-first AI-assisted maternal triage and referral support

**Stage:** Working MVP / early advanced MVP

MatriSense helps pregnant mothers report symptoms in Bangla, converts those reports into structured triage cases, checks maternal danger signs through a rule-based safety layer, retrieves guideline-grounded care guidance through rule-aware RAG, and sends high-risk cases to health workers for follow-up and referral.

The product is not an AI doctor. It is a safety-first bridge between rural mothers, family caregivers, health workers, and referral facilities.
