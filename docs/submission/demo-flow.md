# MatriSense Demo Flow

## Patient Side

1. Register/login as a mother.
2. Complete or edit profile with district, upazila, address, and pregnancy stage.
3. Start triage.
4. Enter Bangla symptoms, for example: `আমার মাথা খুব ব্যথা করছে আর চোখে ঝাপসা দেখছি`.
5. Confirm extracted symptoms.
6. Answer follow-up questions.
7. View risk result and safe Bangla guidance.
8. View triage history.

## Health Worker Side

1. Login as health worker.
2. View regional case dashboard.
3. Filter by district/status/risk.
4. Open high-risk case detail.
5. Review patient profile/location, symptoms, rule reasons, RAG guidance, and audit history.
6. Assign or reassign hospital.
7. Update case status.

## Main Story

MatriSense turns unstructured Bangla symptom reports into safe, structured maternal triage and connects high-risk mothers to human health workers for follow-up.
